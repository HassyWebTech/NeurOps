How it works

Three steps from raw data to clear business intelligence.

Step 01

Upload your business data

Connect your customer records, order history, reviews, or CSV files. NeurOps ingests them and creates vector embeddings stored in Qdrant.

Step 02

Ask in plain English

Type any business question naturally. The agent retrieves the most relevant data using semantic search — not keyword matching.

Step 03

Get actionable insights

Llama 3.3 70B reads the retrieved context and generates a clear, specific answer with source citations.
